Privacy policy

What we collect

connectunited.guide is a static informational website. We do not operate user accounts, collect personally identifiable information, or run contact forms. Standard web server logs (IP address, page visited, timestamp, browser type) may be retained by our hosting provider, Cloudflare Pages, per their data retention policies.

Analytics

If web analytics are implemented on this site, they will be configured to minimize personal data collection — using aggregate traffic data only, with IP anonymization enabled. No cross-site tracking or advertising pixels are used on this site.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Cloudflare may set security-related cookies as part of its CDN and protection services.

Third-party links

This site links to third-party platforms (Etherscan, Trustpilot, CoinMarketCap, and others). Once you follow an external link, that site's privacy policy applies. We are not responsible for third-party data practices.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the contact information available on the site. For Connect United account data requests, contact Connect United directly at support.connectunited.com.
