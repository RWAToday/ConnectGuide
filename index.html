What is Connect United?

A blockchain platform where participation is the product

Connect United runs the WIN Blockchain — a live Layer-1 network with node operators worldwide. Its model: members learn, complete verified actions, earn on-chain credentials, and node owners govern how the network evolves.

Connect United is a blockchain education and participation platform built on the WIN Blockchain, a live Layer-1 network. Its blockchain is publicly verifiable at win.elevatescan.com. The ERC-20 token contract is auditable on Etherscan. Governance documents are publicly available at win.node-governance.io. It has 96 reviews on Trustpilot averaging 4.8 stars and 98% bullish sentiment from over 7,900 CoinMarketCap votes. This is not financial advice — always verify independently.

Each week, a new lesson is posted covering blockchain, AI, or RWA topics. Members watch or read the lesson, complete a short quiz, take a related action step, and submit verification. Completion is recorded as an NFT on the WIN Blockchain — a permanent, on-chain credential. BlockBot node owners receive weekly lesson access. Droid owners receive monthly access. See the full Learn2Earn guide for more detail.

Both are node products that operate on the WIN Blockchain. BlockBots are the primary node tier — they receive weekly Learn2Earn access and full governance voting rights under the DGF Charter. Droids are an entry-level option with monthly lesson access. Both participate in the Proof of Action reward model. See the nodes guide for a full comparison.

Three independent sources let you verify without relying on Connect United's own website:

  • win.elevatescan.com — real-time block production, wallet counts, and transaction history on the WIN Layer-1
  • Etherscan — the ERC-20 token contract, holder count, and transfer history
  • win.node-governance.io — the Distributed Governance Framework Charter and governance records

WIN has a fixed maximum supply of 50 billion tokens — no additional issuance beyond this hard cap. Distribution occurs through Proof of Action, meaning participation in the network earns tokens rather than passive holding. Built-in halving mechanisms govern emission schedules; any changes require a governance vote by node owners through the DGF. The tokenomics are publicly documented and independently verifiable.
