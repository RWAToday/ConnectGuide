Your assets: Connect United cannot access your wallet

This is worth stating clearly because it is often misunderstood. WIN tokens — whether on the Layer-1 or as ERC-20 — are held in non-custodial wallets. That means:

  • Your private key controls your wallet. Connect United does not hold your key.
  • Connect United cannot access, move, freeze, or confiscate your tokens.
  • If Connect United ceased to exist tomorrow, your on-chain assets would remain in your wallet.
  • On-chain credentials (NFTs earned through Learn2Earn) belong to your wallet address — not to a Connect United account that could be suspended.

This is fundamentally different from a custodial platform (like an exchange) where the company holds your funds. With non-custodial wallets, the blockchain is the ledger — not Connect United's database. The wallet and rewards guide covers this in more detail, including how to verify your balance independently.

The flip side: if you lose your seed phrase, no one can recover your wallet — not Connect United, not anyone. This is why seed phrase backup is critical from day one.

Real risks to understand — even if it is not a scam

Saying Connect United is not a scam is not the same as saying it is risk-free. Anyone doing serious DYOR should understand these legitimate risks before participating:

  • Token price volatility. WIN token value fluctuates with broader crypto market conditions. Rewards earned in WIN may be worth more or less in fiat terms depending on market timing.
  • Nodes must be online to earn. An offline node generates no rewards. You need to either self-host your node or use a managed service like Codex. This is an ongoing operational consideration, not a one-time purchase.
  • Governance risk. Node owner votes could change blockchain distribution parameters in ways that reduce earning rates. Decentralized governance means collective decisions that may not favor every participant equally.
  • Regulatory uncertainty. Crypto regulation varies by jurisdiction and is actively evolving. Participating in a blockchain reward program may have tax and legal implications depending on where you are located.
  • Liquidity risk. WIN may have lower liquidity than major cryptocurrencies. Converting large holdings may affect price.

None of these risks make Connect United a scam. They are standard considerations for any blockchain participation decision — and understanding them is what DYOR is for.
